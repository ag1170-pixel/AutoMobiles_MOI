Toyota Prius

Toyota Prius is as an outstandingly stylish and one of the most exclusive designs on Indian streets.

   
The lofty bumper and the deficiency of a huge grille give the car a very tidy front appeal. Besides being aesthetically sound, Prius has a lot of expertise behind its mechanism. The car's exterior is implausible in context of aerodynamics and the standards implemented are not solely for the performance but also for the environmental benefits.

Features of Toyota Prius

  • New hybrid vehicle and saw its success on European and Japanese roads now in India
  • This is the hybrid concept with luxurious looks and eye catching interiors
  • With hybrid, the models will be available with primary fuels
  • 1.8 litres capacity with rear disk brakes, tilt steering, good ground clearance plus tax benefit in India makes this sedan a tough
  • competitor to civic hybrid already running and mark the era of hybrids in India.
