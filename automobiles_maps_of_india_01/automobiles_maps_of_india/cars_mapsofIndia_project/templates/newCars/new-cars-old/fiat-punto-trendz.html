Fiat Punto Trendz

The Fiat Punto Trendz is credited in bringing a change is the hatchback car scenario in India. Fiat Punto Trendz is just another feather to the hat of the Italian automobile giants Fiat. The Trendz is the latest in the Punto series. The Fiat Punto Trendz is an absolute treat for Fiat lovers. The Fiat Punto Trendz is amazingly attractive and comfortable only giving reasons enough to the Indian citizens to welcome it with open arms. The interior of the car is inspired by the Fiat linea and hence sport an élan that is unmatched in its segment so far. As the name suggest the car is a trendy hatchback mid sized four wheeler offering superior mileage quite unlike other Fiat models.

   

Features of Fiat Punto Trendz



  • Elegant Italian styling by its interior and exterior design.
  • Fiat Grand Punto is extremely spacious
  • It has a large boot space
  • Is incorporated with lots of air bags
  • New three point seat belts with pre-tensioners and load limiters
