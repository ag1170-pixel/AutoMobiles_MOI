Mahindra Xylo Celebration

Mahindra Xylo Celebration is the latest car that has been unleashed by the automobile manufacturer of India, Mahindra and Mahindra. Draped in pearl white color, this new car of India has many exciting features; some features are similar to that of the Xylo E6 model. This luxurious car has been launched for completing a year of the Multi Utility Vehicle of Mahindra and Mahindra. This car is the biggest automobile launched by Mahindra after Mahindra Scorpio.

   

Mahindra Xylo Celebration



  • Mahindra Xylo is a MUV from Mahindra's Stable, Much anticipated a family car in India , this vehicle has enormous features in terms of interiors and exteriors and has seating capacity of 6-8 persons. The inner sittings are so adjusted that you would have a wonderful MUV & a family car
  • With a price range of 7 lacs onwards, The MUV is much in demand with mileage of 11-12 kmpl in city and 14-16 kmpl on highways
