Automobile World Show

This year, the Automobile World Show will take place from July 05, 2012 to July 08, 2012. It is a top-notch event which is collectively hosted by the Government of Gujarat and K and D Communication Limited who also hosted ENGIMACH. The Automobile World Show will demonstrate the advancements and emergence of the vehicle manufacturing industry in the state of Gujarat.

At present, Gujarat ranks among the most technologically developed states in India. The state has carved its niche as an industry powerhouse and is drawing a large number of domestic and international corporations for investing in the state.

The state represents 17% of the overall fixed capital of the country. The industrial domain creates the mainstay of the economic development of Gujarat. When the state is well set to develop additionally as an investor -friendly state, the vehicle manufacturing sector has set the pitch to better its moneymaking prospects.

During the upcoming years, Gujarat will be established as the latest automobile hub in India. The state provides a cost-efficient production base for car manufacturing organizations and the dream of Gujarat will come to fruition with the forthcoming automobile show.

Venue for Automobile World Show

The venue of the show will be the University Grounds, located in the western Indian state of Gujarat. The grounds are renowned for hosting exhibitions and events of varied magnitude. The key features of the show will include:

  • Car manufacturers
  • Electrical Vehicles
  • Concept cars
  • Motorbikes
  • Eco-friendly vehicles

Visitor’s Profile of Automobile World Show

Attendees at the show will include:

  • Industrialists
  • Vehicle manufacturers
  • Exporters and importers of automobiles and their components
  • Vehicle and component distributors
  • Government departments
  • R and D professionals
  • Buyers
  • Retailers
