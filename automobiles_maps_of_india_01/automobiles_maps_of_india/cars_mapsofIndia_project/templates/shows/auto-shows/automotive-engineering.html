Automotive Engineering Show in Pune

Experience the future of automobile technology

Event Details

Automotive Engineering Show has been scheduled to take place from April 26 to April 28, 2012. It is a famous event which is devoted to the automobile sector in India. The venue of this prestigious event is the Auto Cluster Exhibition Centre, Chinchwad, Pune.

This event will take place at the same time with the Excellence Award for Innovation and Creative Automation and will draw guests and prospective buyers from different parts of India and other countries of the world.

Highlights of Automotive Engineering Show

  • World famous fabricators of factory control, automation systems, and measurement
  • Information Technology solutions in development, designing, manufacturing and planning
  • Metal-cutting instruments like laser cutting and machining centers
  • Line builders and assembly line system integrators
  • Focused solutions in welding like laser welding
  • Most up-to-date models like output enhancers, devices, jigs and fittings
  • Metrology, automobile testing, and quality inspection suppliers

Visitor Profile

Representatives from carmakers, automotive parts makers, factory heads, CEOs, general managers and production staff from quality, planning, capital equipments, design, repairs, procurement, research and development experts will visit the event. Popular brands like Tata Motors and Ashok Leyland will grace the occasion.

Organizer

The Organizer of Automotive Engineering Show - Pune is Focussed Event Management Private Limited, which is based in Mumbai, Maharashtra.
