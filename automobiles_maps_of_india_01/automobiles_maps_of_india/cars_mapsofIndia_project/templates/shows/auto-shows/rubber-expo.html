India Rubber Expo 2013

Mumbai, Maharashtra | January 22-24, 2013

Event Overview

India Rubber Expo is set to be the most prestigious event for the Indian rubber industry, fostering collaboration with international participants. With India's booming economy, particularly in the automobile sector, the country has emerged as a major source for rubber goods and a significant marketplace for global buyers.

Date: January 22-24, 2013
Venue: Bombay Exhibition Center (BEC), Mumbai, India
Special Features: Symposium and Buyer-Seller Summit
