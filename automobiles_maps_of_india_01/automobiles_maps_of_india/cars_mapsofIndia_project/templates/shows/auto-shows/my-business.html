My Business India Show 2012

Vapi, Gujarat | May 11-14, 2012

Event Overview

The My Business India Show 2012 is set to be one of the largest business events in Vapi, Gujarat. This four-day extravaganza brings together industry leaders, innovators, and decision-makers under one roof.

Date: May 11-14, 2012
Venue: Vapi Industrial Association Ground, Vapi, Gujarat, India
Expected Attendance: Over 20,000 visitors and 300 exhibitors

Event Highlights

Networking Opportunities

Connect with dealers, manufacturers, and suppliers from various sectors.

Product Showcase

Witness the latest developments in telecommunications, automobile, electrical, and more.

Industry Insights

Gain valuable insights from marketing managers, engineers, and directors.

Business Expansion

Explore new opportunities to increase product salability and expand your network.
