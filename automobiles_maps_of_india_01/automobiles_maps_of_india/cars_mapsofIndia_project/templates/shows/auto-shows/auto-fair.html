Auto Fair Guwahati 2012

September 20-23, 2012 | Maniram Dewan Trade Centre

Auto Fair

This year, the Auto Fair is going to take place in Guwahati, the commercial capital of Assam. It is an important event in Northeast India for the automobile and automation industry.

This is the 3rd version of the Auto Fair and it is being organized by Hype Communications & Marketing Consultancy which also effectively managed the earlier two editions of the event in 2008 and 2011. It will start on September 20, 2012 and conclude on September 23, 2012. The two earlier versions of the show witnessed a considerable number of exhibitors displaying their most recent innovations, drawing a big number of guests.

Venue of Auto Fair

The venue of Auto Fair is the Maniram Dewan Trade Centre, Guwahati, Assam.

Visitor's Profile of Auto Fair

The visitor's profile of Auto Fair Guwahati includes traders, industrialists, consultants, vendors, fabricators, experts, Chief Executive Officers, planners, technical, MDs, technical directors, presidents- projects, clients, financial service providers, investors, research and development firms, factory managers, analysts, project designers, general managers, electrical, mechanical engineers, technicians, electricians, purchase managers, business, general managers, project, government representatives, government and non government contractors, defense spokespersons, railway, power, telecommunications, chemicals, fertilizers, oil refineries, petrochemicals, students and cement plants.
