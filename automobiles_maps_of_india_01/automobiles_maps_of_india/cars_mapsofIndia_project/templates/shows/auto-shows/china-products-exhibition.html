China Products Mumbai India Exhibition

Event Details

Date: December 20-22, 2012

Venue: Bombay Exhibition Centre, Goregaon East, Mumbai (Maharashtra)

Organizer: China Council For the Promotion of International Trade (CCFPIT)

Event Profile

The China Products Mumbai India Exhibition is going to be the largest podium for the industrialists from India and China to meet one another. This is the 9th version of the event and takes place every year. It's an ideal platform for signing contracts, collaborations, and ratifying important agreements between industrialists and entrepreneurs of India and China.

Visitor's Profile

The visitor's profile includes:

  • Exporters and Importers
  • Dealers, Vendors, and Suppliers
  • Franchises
  • High rank corporate executives
  • Retailers
  • Chambers of commerce
  • Government and trade commissions

Exhibitor's Profile

The exhibition encompasses a broad array of products and services including:

  • Construction and Real Estate
  • Interior Decorations
  • Diesel Generator and Engine Sets
  • Makeup and Beauty Products
  • Electronics and Electrical Appliances
  • Food and Beverages
  • Textiles and Apparels
  • Glasswork, Toys, and Gifts
  • Hardware and Tools
  • Handicrafts and Household Appliances
  • Health, Hygiene, and Sanitary Ware
  • Industrial Goods and Water Pumps
  • Stationery and more
