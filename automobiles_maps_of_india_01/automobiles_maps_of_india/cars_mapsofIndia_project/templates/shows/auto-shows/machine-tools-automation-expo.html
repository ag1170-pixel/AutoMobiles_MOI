India Machine Tools and Automation Expo in New Delhi

India Machine Tools and Automation Expo is a perfect place for the manufacturers and suppliers dealing in the machine tools and automation products to come together at a platform and showcase their products before the interested audiences.

Platforms like this not only give an opportunity to the manufacturers and suppliers to come together but they are also very vital for the significant exchange of ideas leading to crucial developments in the sector of automation.

Significant Details about India Machine Tools and Automation Expo

VenueTalkatora Stadium, New Delhi
Date16th -18th March
Important themes for the exhibitionSome of the important themes to be discussed in the exhibition are Boring machines, forging machines, milling machines, tool grinding machines, welding machine, multi-purpose hand tools, abrasive tools, industrial cleaning, tooling and quality assurance, machining centers, material handling equipments and many more.
