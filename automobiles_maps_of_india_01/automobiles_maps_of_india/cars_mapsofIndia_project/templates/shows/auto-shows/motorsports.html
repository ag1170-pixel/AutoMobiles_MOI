The Powerhouse of Indian Motorsports

The Federation International de l'Automobile (FIA) governs global motorsport, with the Motorsports Association of India (MAI) as its national representative. MAI stands as the sole authority for all motorsport events in India.

India's motorsport market is now valued at an impressive ₹20 crores, with major corporations like Tata and Maruti actively championing the sport.

Evolution of Indian Motorsports

From its humble beginnings in the 1970s, Indian motorsports has undergone a dramatic transformation. The last decade has seen the emergence of world-class drivers like Narain Karthikeyan, Hari Singh, and Karun Chandhok, putting India on the global racing map.

Premier Racing Events

JK Tyre Motorsports

A pioneer in Indian motorsports, JK Tyres organizes prestigious events including:

  • JK Tyre National Karting Championship
  • JK Tyre National Racing Championship
  • Team India JK Tyre (Formula Asia)

Volkswagen Polo Cup

Launched in 2010, the Volkswagen Polo Cup has quickly become one of India's most anticipated racing series, featuring six high-octane races from June to December.
