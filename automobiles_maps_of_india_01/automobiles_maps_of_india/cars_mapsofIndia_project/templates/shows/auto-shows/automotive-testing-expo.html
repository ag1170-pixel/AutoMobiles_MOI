Automotive Testing Expo India 2024

Automotive Expo boasts of being India's only automotive testing, evaluation and quality engineering trade fair. The fair gives a common platform to the visitors and exhibitors to come in direct contact to each other and make the maximum of this rare technological extravaganza. The automotive Expo 2012 gives that rare occasion where buyers, sellers and the product come face to face.

Details on Automotive Expo 2024

Venue Chennai Trade and Convention Centre
Date 6th March-8th March 2024
Product Area Test simulation, Engine and emissions analysis, Vibration testing, Acoustic testing, Environmental testing, Mechanical testing, Data acquisition, Materials testing, Non-destructive testing (NDT), Track simulation and laboratory testing, EMC analysis, Structural and fatigue testing, Suspension kinematics and compliance, Simulation software, Sensors and transducers, On-board diagnostics, Wind tunnel technology.
Major Participants Ashok Leyland, Daimler India, Ford India, Hyundai Motor Engineering
Entry Fee The entry to the event is absolutely free. However, one is required to register at the official website to get free official entry badge.
Official Hotel Hotel Le Meridien, Chennai
Authorised Website http://www.testing-expo.com/india/
Must Visit for Automotive Test Engineers
Number of exhibitors 120
Who can visit the event Trade visitors only

Major Attractions

The major attractions of this event include ATS technology forum which can be of great use for engineers from fields like Vehicle Testing, RLDA, Component testing, Remote data logging, Hybrid or Electric Vehicle development, NVH, Tire Development and Robotics. Visitors will also get to attend technology workshops every morning.
