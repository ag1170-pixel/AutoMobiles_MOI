Partmart & Autocare

The Partmart & Autocare will take place from February 15, 2013 to February 17, 2013 in New Delhi, India. The organizer of this event is the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII).

Event Profile of Partmart & Autocare

This is the sixth version of Partmart & Autocare and this event will emphasize on services, automobile aftermarket, spare parts, components, devices and support services.

The exhibition offers an outstanding chance to the automobile aftermarket participants in India and also in the Asian territory for getting entry to the fast developing automobile aftermarket domain in India.
