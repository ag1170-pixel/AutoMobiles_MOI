Used Car Buying Tips

  1. Engine: Check for single reboring, it may lower value by ₹10,000 - ₹20,000.
  2. Tyres: Inspect for wear, budget ₹2,500 per tyre if needed.
  3. Chassis: Look for accident signs on undercarriage and bodyline.
  4. Battery: Factor ₹2,500 for replacement if not new.
  5. Documentation: Verify all necessary papers thoroughly.

Required Documentation

  • Car Registration Papers
  • RTO Tax Receipt
  • Road Tax Papers
  • Car Insurance
  • Original Invoice
  • NOC (if bought on finance)
  • Form 35 (if applicable)
